Links to Related Resources

Suny Algorithm Repository Here you will find descriptions of hundreds of problems and their algorithms, with links to existing codes and quality ratings. code
The DIMACS Implementation Challenges A quasi-annual competition to find the best algorithms for stated problems. This repository contains problem specifications, instances and generators, challenges, and some research results.
INFORMS OR/MS Resource Collection Formerly known as Mike Trick's Operations Research Page, a huge source of programs, articles, newsgroups and much more relating to problems of interest in Operations Research.
The Stanford GraphBase Don Knuth's collection of graphs, graph generators, demonstration software, and other resources for problems on graphs. Assignment, biconnected components, shortest paths, minimum spanning trees, and more.
ACM Journal of Experimental Algorithmics High-quality articles on state of the art research in experimental algorithmics.
The R Project for Statistical Computing Download R, a free software environment for statistical computing and graphics.
Graph Coloring Page Joseph Culberson's page for all things relating to Graph Coloring.
A Bibliography of Algorithm Experimentation Originally published in Goldwasser, Johnson, and McGeoch, eds, Data Structures, Near Neighbor Searches, and Methodology: Fifth and Sixth DIMACS Implementation Challenges, DIMACS Series in Discrete Mathematics and Theoretical Computer Science, Volume 59, American Mathematical Society, 2002.
